Welcome to my portfolio! I am a second-year engineering student at BVDUET NM, studying Computer Science.

I am passionate about technology and its applications. In addition to my academic pursuits, I also have a keen interest in drawing and painting. I find the creative process both fulfilling and refreshing.

Throughout my academic journey, I have been involved in projects that have allowed me to apply my knowledge and skills in practical settings. I am eager to continue learning and growing in this dynamic field.

Outside of academics, you can often find me with a sketchbook or canvas, exploring different techniques and expressing my creativity through art. Feel free to browse my portfolio to learn more about my projects and experiences!
